Information we collect

During the registration process you have entered your email and mobile number. To tip someone using our service you have also entered a credit card number. After finishing the registration process, the application has asked your permission to access your addressbook.
This is the only information we collect from you.

How we use information we collect

The information that we collect is only used to enable you to use the application with your friends.
Your addressbook information is used to find friends for you that are also using the application, so they will show up in your friend list.
Your email address will be used if you request to reset your password. We will only use your credit card information to conduct transactions that you have approved.

Information we share

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations and individuals, unless for legal reasons, after we have been served a warrant.
